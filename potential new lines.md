# Potential New Lines

Every original line is followed by its verdict and, where applicable, a suggested rewrite.

## Lorenzo

**Original:** THE PIPES HERE ARE DECORATIVE. IT DISGUSTS ME.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Perfectly establishes his moral outrage about fake plumbing.

**Original:** I BROUGHT A TROMBONE. FOR PLUMBING.
**Verdict:** KEEP — A strong, concise misuse of a pipe-shaped object.

**Original:** THE PRETZEL STAND SERVES ONLY SOUP NOW. I RESPECT THE PIVOT.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — The business observation isn't specific enough to his plumbing obsession.
**Rewrite:** THE PRETZEL STAND SERVES SOUP THROUGH A TAP. CRUDE, BUT HONEST.

**Original:** I ORDERED A CALZONE. IT ARRIVED AS A DECORATIVE PIPE. I ATE IT ANYWAY.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Eating it undermines his disgust for decorative pipes.
**Rewrite:** I ORDERED A CALZONE. IT ARRIVED AS A DECORATIVE PIPE. I SENT IT BACK ON PRINCIPLE.

**Original:** THE FOOD COURT HAS NO PROPER DRAINAGE. I HAVE FILED A COMPLAINT WITH MYSELF.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Strong combination of professional seriousness and absurd bureaucracy.

**Original:** SOMEONE CALLED MY WRENCH A PROP. WE ARE NOT SPEAKING.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Concise, wounded and completely specific to Lorenzo.

**Original:** I FIXED THE VENDING MACHINE. IT NOW ONLY DISPENSES REGRET.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — "Dispenses regret" is generic absurdism that could belong to anyone.
**Rewrite:** I FIXED THE VENDING MACHINE. IT NOW DRAINS CORRECTLY. IT DISPENSES NOTHING.

**Original:** STANDARD PLUMBING PROCEDURE DOES NOT COVER FOOD COURTS. I AM IMPROVISING.
**Verdict:** KEEP — His trade being applied far outside its proper scope works well.

## Gnash

**Original:** I FINISHED TALKING TO YOU YESTERDAY. YOU ARE JUST NOW ARRIVING.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Strong temporal absurdity built directly around his speed.

**Original:** THE SODA MACHINE DISPENSED ONE PERFECT GRAPE. I DRANK IT.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — The grape is random and unrelated to his speed or waiting problem.
**Rewrite:** THE SODA MACHINE IS STILL DISPENSING MY DRINK. I FINISHED IT TEN MINUTES AGO.

**Original:** RUN FASTER. OR AT ALL. EITHER IS FINE.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Short, impatient and recognizably his voice.

**Original:** I ARRIVED SO EARLY I HAD TO WAIT FOR MYSELF TO SHOW UP.
**Verdict:** KEEP — One of his strongest lines.

**Original:** THE PRETZEL LINE MOVES TOO SLOWLY. I HAVE ALREADY EATEN THREE, MENTALLY.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — "Mentally" weakens the physical speed premise.
**Rewrite:** THE PRETZEL LINE MOVES TOO SLOWLY. I HAVE ALREADY REACHED TOMORROW'S LINE.

**Original:** SPIN DASHING THROUGH A FOOD COURT IS FROWNED UPON. I DID IT ANYWAY.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — This is mainly reckless behavior, rather than arriving early and waiting for reality.
**Rewrite:** I REACHED THE END OF THE FOOD COURT BEFORE THEY PUT UP THE WARNING SIGN.

**Original:** I BEAT THE HIGH SCORE ON THE ARCADE CABINET BEFORE IT FINISHED BOOTING.
**Verdict:** KEEP — A very clean impossible-speed variation.

**Original:** WAITING IS A SKILL. I HAVE MASTERED IT OUT OF NECESSITY.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — It explains his situation rather than turning it into a joke.
**Rewrite:** I HAVE MASTERED WAITING. IT TOOK FOREVER.

## Fernwick

**Original:** MY PROPHECY MENTIONS A "BUY ONE GET ONE" EVENT. DARK TIMES.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Excellent interpretation of retail language as prophecy.

**Original:** THE RECEIPT FADES FURTHER EVERY DAY. AS DO WE ALL.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Concise and appropriately grandiose.

**Original:** I HAVE PREPARED FOR THIS. THE RECEIPT SAID TO.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Too generic; it needs visibly mundane receipt language.
**Rewrite:** I HAVE PREPARED FOR THIS. THE RECEIPT SAID "KEEP FROZEN."

**Original:** THE RECEIPT NOW LISTS A COUPON. I DO NOT KNOW FOR WHAT.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Good premise, but the second sentence could provide a stronger turn.
**Rewrite:** THE PROPHECY CONTAINS A COUPON. OUR DESTINY EXPIRES THURSDAY.

**Original:** MY PROPHECY WARNED OF A LONG LINE. IT WAS RIGHT, AS ALWAYS.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Clearly on-bit, even if less surprising than some of the others.

**Original:** THE INK IS ALMOST GONE. SO IS MY PATIENCE.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Repeats the fading receipt and ends with generic impatience.
**Rewrite:** THE LAST LEGIBLE WORD IS "TOTAL." I FEAR THE FINAL RECKONING.

**Original:** I ASKED THE RECEIPT IF WE WIN. IT PRINTED A BARCODE.
**Verdict:** KEEP — A barcode serving as an oracle's answer works well.

**Original:** SHIELD ROLLING PAST THE SALAD BAR FEELS PROPHETIC, SOMEHOW.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — The receipt is absent, and "somehow" makes him sound uncertain about his own bit.
**Rewrite:** THE PROPHECY SAYS "SALAD ROLLS." I HAVE INTERPRETED THIS AS A MANEUVER.

## B-33P

**Original:** STATUS: OPERATIONAL. CYAN: LOW. MORALE: ADEQUATE.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Ideal diagnostic voice and a clean introduction to the cyan gag.

**Original:** THE VACUUM CLEANED MY BOOT SECTOR. I FEEL SEEN.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Fun robot wordplay, but it could use the corporate diagnostic voice and cyan obsession.
**Rewrite:** MAINTENANCE COMPLETE. BOOT SECTOR: CLEAN. CYAN: INEXPLICABLY LOWER.

**Original:** UPDATE AVAILABLE. IT WILL NOT INSTALL. THIS IS FINE.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — A generic software joke without B-33P's specific running gag.
**Rewrite:** UPDATE AVAILABLE. INSTALLATION BLOCKED BY LOW CYAN. REQUIREMENT UNEXPLAINED.

**Original:** CYAN LEVEL: CRITICAL. THIS HAS BEEN TRUE FOR YEARS.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Similar to the first line, but "for years" provides a worthwhile new turn.

**Original:** SCANNING FOOD COURT. RESULT: FOOD. COURT. CONFIRMED.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Strong literal diagnostic processing.

**Original:** MY LEMON CANNON REQUIRES MAINTENANCE. MY LEMON CANNON WILL NOT RECEIVE IT.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — The cannon fits, but the denial should connect to the cyan gag.
**Rewrite:** LEMON CANNON MAINTENANCE DENIED. REASON: LOW CYAN. THE CANNON IS YELLOW.

**Original:** ERROR 404: MORALE NOT FOUND. REPORTING ADEQUATE ANYWAY.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — "Error 404" is familiar, and morale already appears in the first line.
**Rewrite:** PERFORMANCE REVIEW COMPLETE. ALL FAILURES ATTRIBUTED TO CYAN SUPPLY.

**Original:** THE PRINTER NEXT DOOR AND I HAVE MUCH IN COMMON. NEITHER OF US HAS CYAN.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Direct, specific and fully committed to the central gag.

**Original:** POYO POYO.

## Gary

**Original:** HR SAYS BEING DECEASED IS NOT APPROVED LEAVE. I HAVE APPEALED.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Perfectly defines Gary's premise.

**Original:** MY COWORKERS SENT A FAREWELL CARD. IT SAYS "SEE YOU MONDAY."
**Verdict:** KEEP — Strong bureaucratic mishandling of his death.

**Original:** THE PAWN SHOP IS LEGALLY DISTINCT. FROM WHAT? EXACTLY.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — "Legally distinct" is vague and unrelated to his employment-status dispute.
**Rewrite:** THE PAWN SHOP STILL LISTS ME AS EMERGENCY CONTACT. THIS HAS CAUSED DELAYS.

**Original:** I AM THE ONLY ENTITY IN THIS ROOM RESPONSIBLE FOR THE PHYSICAL TOGGLE SWITCHES. THE REST OF YOU ARE LITERALLY CONSTRUCTED FROM MATH.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — At 132 characters, it exceeds the limit and becomes a fourth-wall observation about digital construction.
**Rewrite:** I AM STILL RESPONSIBLE FOR THE PHYSICAL SWITCHES. DEATH DID NOT UPDATE THE ROSTER.

**Original:** HR SAYS LOGGING INTO A DIGITAL ENVIRONMENT DOES NOT CONSTITUTE A COMMUTE. MY TIME-CARD IS COMPLICATED.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Under the limit, but cumbersome and close to fourth-wall territory.
**Rewrite:** HR SAYS HAUNTING THE OFFICE DOES NOT COUNT AS A COMMUTE. MY TIME-CARD DISAGREES.

**Original:** THE VENDING MACHINE UNIONIZED WITHOUT ME. I FEEL LEFT OUT.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Generic workplace material without the deceased-status element.
**Rewrite:** THE VENDING MACHINE UNION REJECTED ME. APPARENTLY I AM NO LONGER LABOR.

**Original:** MY DESK IS STILL RESERVED. I APPRECIATE THE GESTURE, HR.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Nicely mundane, melancholy and bureaucratic.

**Original:** I HAUNT THE PAWN SHOP DURING BUSINESS HOURS ONLY. IT IS POLICY.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Very clean expression of the ghost/employee combination.

**Original:** THE FAREWELL CARD HAD A TYPO. I AM STILL UPSET ABOUT IT.
**Verdict:** REWRITE — Repeats the farewell-card subject without adding much.
**Rewrite:** MY DEATH CERTIFICATE MISSPELLED MY NAME. HR SAYS I MAY STILL BE ALIVE.

**Original:** TECHNICALLY I NEVER CLOCKED OUT. LEGALLY THIS IS A GRAY AREA.
**Verdict:** KEEP — Excellent legal-bureaucratic closer.
